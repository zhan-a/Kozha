What we offer

Built for real classrooms
and real conversations.

Voice-to-Sign

Record speech directly in your browser. Transcription happens locally — no audio is sent anywhere. Completely free and private.

Text-to-Sign

Type any sentence and watch it translated into sign language animations in real time.

Video Lectures

Upload a recorded video and Bridgn extracts the audio, transcribes it, and sequences the sign translation automatically.

STEM Vocabulary

Pre-loaded with scientific abbreviation packs. "Carbon dioxide" becomes "CO₂" before planning — no manual editing needed.

Instant Planning

Your text is automatically processed and mapped to sign language word order — no manual editing needed.

Open & Extendable

Community volunteers can contribute new signs and translations directly. The more people contribute, the richer it gets.

The process

Three steps.
Zero friction.

01
Input your words

Type text, record your voice, or upload a video file. Bridgn supports all three input modes in a single session.

02
Smart processing

Your text is processed and matched against our sign language database to find the best possible translation for each word and phrase.

03
Live sign animation

The translated signs are played as a smooth animation. Everything runs in your browser — no server processing, no delays.

Help us grow the dictionary.

Bridgn's sign database is built by volunteers. If you know sign language, work in education, or just want to help, you can contribute new signs, test translations, or report gaps — right from your browser.
