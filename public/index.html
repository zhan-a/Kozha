Bridgn translates typed text, recorded speech, and uploaded video into sign-language animations — entirely in your browser, free and open-source.

  • Sign-language databases: DictaSign (BSL, GSL/LSF), SignAvatars (DGS, PJM), SignLanguageSynthesis (NGT), algerianSignLanguage-avatar, bdsl-3d-animation (Bangla), Text-to-Sign-Language and text_to_isl (Indian), KurdishSignLanguage, VSL (Vietnamese), syntheticfsl and signtyper (Filipino).
